Get the free toolkit

Why Radical Product?

In the last decade, methodologies such as Lean Startup and Agile have helped us innovate faster and move financial KPI up and to the right. But our products haven’t always created a better world and we’ve learned to accept that being successful and changing the world (for the better) are mutually exclusive. We need a new way of thinking to break through this dichotomy.

Vision-driven organizations around the world combine Radical Product Thinking with Lean and Agile to align their teams, make smarter decisions, and build products with purpose.

To get started, just download the free Radical Product Toolkit and use the framework with your team to define a compelling vision and strategy which drives prioritization, execution, and measurement. If you need hands-on help, it’s just an email away.

Who's Behind Radical Product?

What started as a framework for building successful products has now become a global movement of leaders creating purpose-driven change through their products. Radhika Dutt is a product leader and entrepreneur who created the toolkit together with Geordie Kaytes and Nidhi Aggarwal in 2017.
